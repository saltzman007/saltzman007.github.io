Words of wisdom

"My work explores the relationship between emerging sexualities and emotional memories. With influences as diverse as Wittgenstein and Ornette Coleman, new variations are synthesised from both orderly and random discourse. Ever since I was a teenager I have been fascinated by the theoretical limits of the moment. What starts out as triumph soon becomes corrupted into a dialectic of lust, leaving only a sense of nihilism and the inevitability of a new reality. As spatial forms become transformed through boundaried and personal practice, the viewer is left with a hymn to the possibilities of our existence."
Harry Saltzman
